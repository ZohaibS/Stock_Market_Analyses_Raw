Stock Market Analysis Overview

    Stock market analysis has been in-focus due to fluctuations that cause ripple effects on the economy, both positive and negative. Since analysis tools have been more in the fore-front, various investigations have been completed to understand future market fluctuations via stock prices and stability. For this analysis, data consisting of the Dow Jones Inductrial Index and deep larning via Recursive Neural Networks (RNN) and Topological Data Analysis (TDA) were applied. Additionaly, another approach was applied using Direct Signal Processing (DSP) as a means of comparison to machine learning techniques. At the outset to help understand and visualize market prediction, the following questions are to be investigated:


  • What might be the short-term stock future?

  • How many days per time-period did the DJIA take a large hit (2% change)?

Dow Jones Industrial Average (DJIA) - the DOW

  • DOW is a stock market index composed of 30 large, publicly owned companies based in the United States, 30 "blue-chip" stocks.
  • The current market cap: $7.5 trillion (Sep. 2018).
  • Trading occurs on the NYSE and NASDAQ under the symbol DJI.
  • The DJIA is the oldest continuous barometer of the U.S. stock market, and the most widely quoted indicator of U.S. stock market activity.
